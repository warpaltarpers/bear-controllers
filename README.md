# Bear Controllers
TeensyLC .ino files for the bear controllers in Rainbow Bear Attack

## TeensyLC Pin and Control Layouts
### Player 1
PIN | KEY | CONTROL
--- | --- | -------
0 | A | Move Left
1 | W | Move Right
2 | D | Jump
14 | E | Attack

### Player 2
PIN | KEY | CONTROL
--- | --- | -------
0 | J | Move Left
1 | I | Move Right
2 | L | Jump
14 | P | Attack
